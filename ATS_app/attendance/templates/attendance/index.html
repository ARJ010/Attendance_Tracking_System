Attendance Tracking System

Seamless, accurate, and efficient student attendance management.

About ATS

The Attendance Tracking System (ATS) is designed for effortless student attendance tracking in the Four-Year UG Programme. It ensures accuracy while reducing manual effort for instructors.

Why ATS?

- Simple and intuitive interface for seamless tracking.
- Automated reports for real-time insights.
- Ensures student accountability and performance monitoring.

Key Features

✔ Instant Attendance: Mark attendance in seconds.

✔ Detailed Reports: Generate and export attendance reports.

✔ Notifications: Alerts for students with low attendance.

✔ Course Organization: Manage courses, enroll students, and assign instructors.
